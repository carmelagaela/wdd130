Cagay River Rafting Site

The primary purpose of Cagay River Rafting site is to give detailed information about our rafting company and what we have to offer. Visitors can research for long and short rafting experiences for groups and individuals. They will also find answers to their commonly asked questions and our contact information to contact us and schedule their rafting trip. Our target audience is individuals and groups who are river lovers, regardless of age as long as they are able to raft, wants to have a fun and safe activity. We provide a short-time service for individuals who want to experience a safe rafting adventure but don't want to pay, or have the space, and have themselves a rafting equipment get up for long term. We aim to bring excellent and safe rafting experience for you and your group!

History

Cagay River Rafting was founded in 2018 by Carmela Gaela with a passion for exploring rivers and providing thrilling adventures. Starting small, the company grew steadily, expanding its offerings to include challenging routes and attracting adventurers regionally. Key milestones include introducing multi-day expeditions and earning recognition for safety and environmental stewardship. Today, Cagay River is a leader in the industry, known for its innovative techniques, community involvement, and commitment to sustainable tourism in the Philippines.
